### License to ARKA Engine

Customer grants ARKA Systems LLC a limited license to:

1. **Process Data:** Process customer data to provide the service
2. **Store Data:** Store customer data as necessary for service operation
3. **Display:** Display customer content back to authorized users
4. **Analyze:** Analyze anonymized, aggregated data for service improvement

This license is:
- Non-exclusive
- Royalty-free
- Limited to service provision
- Revocable upon service termination
- Subject to data protection obligations

Customer represents and warrants that:

1. Customer owns or has rights to all submitted content
2. Content does not infringe third-party rights
3. Content complies with applicable laws
4. Customer has authority to grant licenses herein
